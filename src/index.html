About Us

Experienced. Certified. Committed to Your Confidence.

Jim, the owner and lead inspector, brings years of hands-on RV experience to every inspection. Based in Portland, Oregon, Jim has completed over 400 inspections since earning his NRVIA certification in 2022. His deep knowledge of RV systems, combined with his passion for helping people make informed decisions, ensures that every customer gets a thorough, unbiased report they can trust.

What sets Jim apart is his unique approach — not only does he identify potential problems, but he also provides estimated repair costs. This added insight gives buyers and sellers a valuable tool for negotiation.

Whether you’re buying your first RV, preparing to sell, or filing a warranty or insurance claim, Jim is dedicated to providing the knowledge and confidence you need to make smart decisions. Serving all of Washington and Oregon, every inspection is more than just a checklist — it’s your roadmap to buying, selling, and owning with confidence.
