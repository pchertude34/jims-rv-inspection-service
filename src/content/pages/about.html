About Us

Experience You Can Trust. Service You Can Count On.

"Buy (and Negotiate) with Confidence"

At Jim's RV Inspections Services, we believe every RV buyer, seller, and owner deserves clear, honest information before making important decisions. That’s why Jim, our owner and lead inspector, brings his years of hands-on experience and professional training to every inspection.

Based in Portland, Oregon, Jim is a certified NRVIA inspector who has completed over 400 inspections since earning his certification in 2022. His expertise covers everything from essential systems like propane and plumbing to detailed exterior assessments, helping clients get a full picture of an RV’s condition.

But it’s not just about identifying problems — Jim goes the extra mile by including estimated repair costs, giving you valuable context for negotiations, budgeting, or planning your next steps. Whether you’re buying your dream RV, preparing to sell, or filing a warranty or insurance claim, Jim is dedicated to giving you the knowledge and confidence you need.
